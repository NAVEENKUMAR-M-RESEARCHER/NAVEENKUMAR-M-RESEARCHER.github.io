Call for Papers

The International Conference on Quantum, Artificial Intelligence, and Connected Systems (QAIC 2026) invites original research papers describing innovative results and advancements in the following domains:

  • Quantum Computing and Quantum Algorithms
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision
  • IoT, Edge Computing, and Cybersecurity
  • Quantum Communication and Cryptography
  • AI for Healthcare, Robotics, and Smart Cities

Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not under consideration elsewhere. Submissions should follow the conference template (A4, double-column) and be uploaded via EasyChair (link to be announced).
