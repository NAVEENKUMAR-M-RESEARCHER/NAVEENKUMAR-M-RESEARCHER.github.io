About QAIC 2026

The QAIC 2026 conference brings together researchers, practitioners and industry to present advances and discuss challenges in Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence and Connected Systems. The conference encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and real-world impact.

Conference Tracks

  • Quantum Computing and Quantum Communication
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Large Language Models and Generative AI
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Edge Computing
  • Cybersecurity and Quantum Cryptography
  • AI for Healthcare and Smart Systems
  • Cloud and Green Computing
