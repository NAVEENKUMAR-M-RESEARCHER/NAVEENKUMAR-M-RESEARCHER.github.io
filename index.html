About Me

Dr. NaveenKumar Mahamkali is an accomplished academic and researcher, currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SRM University-AP, India. He earned his Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli, where his research focused on human action recognition using skeleton data—an area with profound applications in healthcare, surveillance, and human-computer interaction. With over a decade of academic and research experience, Dr. Mahamkali has cultivated expertise in deep learning, medical image analysis, microarray and RNA-Seq data classification, and computer vision. His Ph.D. journey ignited a passion for developing intelligent systems that interpret human activity, and this passion now extends to advancing AI-driven solutions in medical imaging and life sciences.

He has authored and co-authored several papers in reputed international journals and conferences, contributing to the growing body of knowledge in AI for healthcare. As an educator, Dr. Mahamkali is deeply committed to mentoring students and young researchers, inspiring them to innovate fearlessly and solve global challenges through technology. His long-term vision is to blend artificial intelligence with healthcare innovation, making diagnostic and predictive systems smarter, faster, and more accessible — thereby impacting lives worldwide.
