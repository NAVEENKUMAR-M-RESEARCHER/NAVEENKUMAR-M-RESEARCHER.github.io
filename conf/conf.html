Secure and Intelligent Edge Networks

Privacy-Preserving AI and Resilient Architectures for IoT and 6G

📅 November 12, 2025 | 📍 NEW2AN 2025

Workshop Overview

This workshop brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to address security, privacy, and AI challenges in 5G/6G and IoT systems. Topics include federated learning, secure communication, cryptography, and adversarial defenses.
